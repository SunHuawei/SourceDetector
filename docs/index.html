What Source Detector does

Source map discovery

Detect source map files referenced by pages and related client-side assets while you browse.

Leak scanning

Use built-in and custom rules to surface risky patterns such as exposed API or AI-related credentials.

Evidence export

Review findings in the explorer and export versions or domain-level bundles as ZIP files.

Who it is for

Security researchers

Speed up recon and prioritize client-side exposure worth investigating.

Developers and front-end teams

Audit exposed assets, review accidental leaks, and inspect what is reachable from production pages.

How it works

1. Browse

Open a target website in Chrome.

2. Collect

Source Detector watches source map references and relevant assets.

3. Analyze

Review evidence, scan for risky patterns, and export artifacts when needed.

Privacy and permissions

Source Detector is designed around a local-first workflow. Core functionality does not require an account. Collected artifacts and settings are intended to stay on your device for analysis workflows.
